The Beauty of Proteas

Scientific Name: Protea cynaroides

Region: Southern Africa

Proteas are a unique and iconic symbol of and national flower of SA, representing the country's rich and diverse flora. These stunning flowers are renowned for their unique and intricate shapes, pink flower heads, and vibrant colours.

Bird of Paradise

Scientific Name: Strelitzia reginae

Region: South Africa

The Bird of Paradise flower resembles a colorful bird in flight. It is popular in ornamental gardens and symbolizes freedom and joy.
